# Data minigames
A Wario ware style game, with a collections of minigames around the theme of data. You play through a series of minigames and try to maximise your score!

Controls: mouse only
# Minigames

- Cats or dogs: you have to tell if a photo is showing a cat or a dog
- Different color: you have to click on the circle with a different color than the others
- Colored text: you have to click on the color described by the word, for example, if the word is "green" (but written in red), you have to click on green, and not red
